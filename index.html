About us

“Security is the greatest wealth. Protect your family’s future with us.”

Mwancelele Company limited was incorporated by The Registrar of Companies on 6TH October 2021 and subsequently registered by Tanzania Revenue Authority as Customs Clearing and Forwarding Agent.

Our services includes

  • Clearling and Forwading.
  • Education services.
  • insurance services
  • Construction of Building and other Engineering Projects
