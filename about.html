Mwancelele Company LTD

Was incorporated by The Registrar of Companies on 6TH October 2021 and subsequently registered by Tanzania Revenue Authority as Customs Clearing and Forwarding Agent.

The company-registered office premises are situated at Pugu station Dra es salaam and with active working office at Makunganya/Simu street-Posta Mpya RITA TOWER 20th Floor   Dar es Salaam.

Our Activities

Mwancelele Company Limited is a locally registered company dealing with the provision of Custom Agency Services and General Port Operation, This is the main activity of the company, We are serving all port clearance services with a quality based on Time and Speed. Experienced staffs make this be a very easy and possible task to make into customers satisfactions. Apart from this, Mwancelele Company limited has its sister companies in business and listed down are the services provided besides CFA activities;

  • Insurance services (under brand name of Mwancelele Insurance Agency), this is done under its sister company under specific brand name, mainly deals with Insurance services on both Life and Non-Life Insurance. Its office is located at Dar es salaam-Tanzania
  • Education Services Mwancelele Primary School, With the same purpose for serving the society through quality services, we preferred to engage our company into education activies as far as giving education is liberating the society. It is located at Mtwara-Tanzania.
  • Engineering, Construction of Buildings & other Civil Engineering Projects

Our Vission

The company strives to achieve customers delight by providing quality freight and logistics service that fully meets their requirements.

Our Mission

The mission of this company is to provide the most reliable, quality, and cost-effective cargo services in order to increase company profitability through its motivated workforce.

Why Choosing Us
